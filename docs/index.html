Self-aligned flange-mounted pumps

Ideal for handling aggressive and explosive liquids such as chlorides, dyes, sulphides, alkalis, and phosphates.

Non-clog pumps

Ideal for pumping slurries or liquids containing fibrous material.

Self-priming pumps

Pumps muddy water, sea water, effluents, and solvents.

Air-cooled thermic fluid pumps

Designed to withstand all kinds of heat transfer fluids over 100°C.

Submerged non-clog pumps

For handling sewage, slurry, viscous liquids, unscreened juice, and textile slurries.

Submerged steam-jacketed pumps

Carries fluids that solidify at ambient temperature and must be heated in transit, e.g. molten sulfur, sulfur trioxide (SO₃).

Submerged multi-stage pumps

Specially designed to give exceptionally high pressure heads.

Floor-mounted multi-stage pumps
